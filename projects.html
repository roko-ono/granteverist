Hydroponics

Data Science

Analyzing trends in bike theft

While working for a micromobility startup in San Francisco, I analyzed open-source data from the SFPD and internal theft data in R to identify high-risk theft scenarios which informed solutions for protecting customers and inventory.

This analysis helped bring our theft rates down from over 40% to under 12% and guided the recovery of over $15,000 in hardware and over $3,000 in unpaid arrears.


Clustering, statistical simulation, and NLP

I used ML to help a local cafe explore purchase history and uncover trends that could be used to improve pricing and bundling, and I scraped and cleaned customer reviews for an emerging hotel chain, performing clustering and sentiment analysis to reveal trends that illuminated aspects of the customer experience where the hotel was falling short.

Analyzing Decentralized Exchange Pools with SQL

Decentralized exchanges (DEXs) are are peer-to-peer marketplaces where cryptocurrency traders can make trades without any intermediary. These transactions are facilitated by self-executing agreements written in code called smart contracts.

DEXs need liquidity to offer accurate market prices on trades, and they attract this liquidity by offering a variety of incentives.

I used SQL to analyze a handful of the top DEXs and made a comparison on Dune Analytics of their popularity. More of my decentralized data analysis work can be found here.

Economic Stability Research

In 2021, Jay Sheth and I set out to research the economic impact of localized crafts for rural communities in the Balkans. Together with communities and officials throughout Germany, Slovenia, Croatia, and Greece, we developed a report and business plan for small businesses and rural communities of Appalachia.

Veera

Addressing food waste

In 2018, I cofounded Veera, a cold food storage solution that enhances the power of evaporative cooling by controlling environmental conditions outside the unit.

We designed a cold storage device that uses solar power to extend the shelf life of produce, dynamically reacting to optimize cooling given the heat and humidity.

We conducted customer discovery interviews with over 100 small-holder farmers, distributors and families in rural Bihar, India and Kathmandu, Nepal. We raised over $10,000 through bootstrapping efforts and received an initial 1000 unit commitment from the Bihar government. My cofounder, Varun, has written more about our work on his site.
