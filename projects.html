/ [2020] Trends in SF Bike Theft
▼

Anyone who's spent time in SF is often all too familiar with theft.

While working for a micromobility startup in San Francisco, I analyzed open-source data from the SFPD and internal theft data in R to identify high-risk theft scenarios which informed solutions for protecting customers and inventory.

This analysis helped bring our theft rates down by over 70% and guided the recovery of over $20,000 in hardware and arrears.

/ [2018] Passive Refrigeration
▼

Cold chain inefficiencies around the globe cause up to 40% of food to spoil before it reaches a plate.

At UNC I cofounded Veera, a cold storage solution with enhanced evaporative cooling.

Working with smallholder farmers and distributors in Bihar and Kathmandu, Nepal, we designed a cold storage unit using solar power to extend the shelf life of produce, dynamically reacting to optimize cooling for any given temperature and humidity. My cofounder, Varun, has written more about the startup.
