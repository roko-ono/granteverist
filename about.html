An overview of my ideas, projects, and the forces that shape them.

Bio

My focus is making systems of trust, power, and decision-making more transparent, resilient and fair.

I'm currently working on this as a researcher and writer for venture-backed crypto infra, apps, and protocols.

My past endeavors include running a successful campaign in North Carolina, building GTM at startups, and studying Economics and Design at UNC.

Based in Brooklyn, NY.

Interests

I have a strong appreciation for nature, technology, and economics. I'm most interested in projects that bring these fields together, including smart contracts, urban agriculture, prediction markets, and micromobility.

Dozens of other solutions addressing economic inequality, climate change, and inefficient government continuously pique my interests.

My interests all tie back to a core curiosity about decision making:

How can we design the world so that the easiest decision is also the best?
